About Isanti Guest Farm

Nestled in the heart of the bushveld, just under two hours from Pretoria, Isanti Guest Farm welcomes you to a malaria-free haven within the Springbok plains. Escape the urban hustle and bustle for a tranquil retreat, offering modern air-conditioned accommodations with complimentary Wi-Fi. Our communal Lapa provides essential self-catering amenities and cutlery, ensuring a seamless stay. Embrace the outdoors with a dedicated braai boma, a well-stocked bar area, and a refreshing swimming pool, creating the perfect setting for quality time with friends and family.

Our farm is a sanctuary for diverse wildlife, including Impala, Blesbuck, Waterbuck, Greater Kudu, Blue Wildebeest, Zebra, Duiker, Warthog, and more. Our Buffalo Breeding program offers an opportunity for an up-close encounter with these majestic creatures.

Explore the rich birdlife on our farm, featuring the African Paradise-Flycatcher, Red-chested Cuckoo, Hornbills, Woodpeckers, Crimson-breasted Shrike, Sunbirds, and more, adding a vibrant touch to the natural surroundings.

Beyond its role as a wildlife haven, Witkop is a dynamic crop farm, cultivating Corn, Sunflower, Peanuts, and more on our arid lands. Additionally, we contribute fresh produce like Watermelon, Hubbard, and Gem Squash to local markets, reflecting our commitment to sustainable farming practices.

Accommodation

Cabins

Discover comfort in three cozy wooden cabins, each hosting four individuals with 1 double bed and 2 single beds. Additionally, find spacious accommodation in a wooden cabin designed for six occupants, offering six comfortable single beds. All units ensure your comfort with ample bathrooms featuring a shower, basin, and toilet. Enhance your stay with modern amenities such as air-conditioning and complimentary Wi-Fi.

Bush Camp

The Bush Camp offers a communal open-plan Lapa that combines a Kitchen, Lounge, and Toilet. The Kitchen is well-equipped with essential amenities such as a Fridge/Freezer, microwave oven, necessary crockery, oven/gas stove, dining table, TV, and free Wi-Fi, providing a comfortable and convenient stay.

Bar & Boma

The Bar and Boma Facilities at our disposal include a convenient fridge/freezer, an ice machine for refreshing beverages, a spacious 10-seater table for gatherings, two well-equipped braai areas, and a cozy fire pit for added warmth and ambiance.

Parking

Experience the convenience of our well-shaded undercover parking facilities during your stay, ensuring your vehicle is protected from the elements while you enjoy peace of mind and easy access to your accommodation.

Activities

Explore Nature

Start your day on a vibrant note by embracing the early morning serenity with a rejuvenating run or walk as the sun gracefully ascends, casting its golden glow. Alternatively, craft an adventurous bush walk with your kids to delve into the wonders of nature. Discover trails that lead you through diverse landscapes, offering glimpses of both small and large animals. Opt for self-drive game drives or enjoy the freedom of exploring on bicycles and motorbikes, as our surroundings welcome enthusiasts of all kinds.

Bird Watching

Explore our bird hides strategically located near waterholes, providing an unparalleled haven for avid bird enthusiasts. Immerse yourself in the captivating symphony of diverse birdlife, creating an enchanting ambiance throughout the camp, perfect for a serene afternoon nap. Just a short 30-minute drive away, the Nylsvlei Nature Reserve awaits your discovery. For those passionate about bird watching, contact us for exclusive pricing details tailored to enhance your avian adventure.

Shooting Range

Experience precision at our extensive shooting facilities, featuring a 300m rifle range, a designated area for pistol enthusiasts, and a space for clay pigeon shooting. Whether you're a seasoned marksman or a novice, enjoy tailored experiences in a safe and controlled environment at Isanti Guest Farm.

Bushveld TV

End your day by immersing yourself in the mesmerizing Bushveld TV, where the flickering flames captivate your thoughts. Let the echoes of the jackals gently bring you back to the present moment. Gaze upward into the crystal-clear sky, where the brilliance of the stars extends as far as the eye can perceive, creating a celestial spectacle to round off your evening.
